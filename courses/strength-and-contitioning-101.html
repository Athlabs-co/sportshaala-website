About the Course
Course Objective

The objective of this module is to provide fundamental understandings of Strength & Conditioning and how to physically prepare athletes.

You will start with the understanding of the role of strength & conditioning, learning the most important training principles, and the basic physiology of sport and exercise.

Finally, the course will provide a basic understanding of the main bio-motor abilities, strength, speed and endurance, and how to effectively train each of these bio-motor abilities.

Along the way, you will operationalize the key concepts of strength training, speed training and endurance training, and how to design training plans.

Learning Objective

After this first course, you will be able to
a) Understand the basic physiology of sport and exercise.
b) Design basic training programs for athletes
c) Tell how well they are performing and monitor their progress
